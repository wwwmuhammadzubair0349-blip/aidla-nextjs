## 2. GEO & AEO Implementation Plan (AI Search Optimization)

To dominate AI Overviews (SGE), ChatGPT, and Perplexity, we will implement:

- **Direct Answer Blocks:** Creating "What is..." and "How to..." sections with concise, 40-60 word answers that AI engines can easily extract as snippets.
- **Entity Linking:** Explicitly linking "CV Builder" $\rightarrow$ "ATS (Applicant Tracking System)" $\rightarrow$ "Job Market (Pakistan/UAE)".
- **Structured Data (JSON-LD):** 
    - `SoftwareApplication` for the tool itself.
    - `FAQPage` for common career questions.
    - `HowTo` for "How to create a winning CV".
    - `Organization` to establish EEAT.
- **Semantic Hierarchy:** Using `H1` $\rightarrow$ `H2` $\rightarrow$ `H3` strictly to define the topical map.

## 3. Technical SEO & UI/UX Roadmap

- **Metadata:** Dynamic, keyword-rich titles and descriptions.
- **Performance:** Zero layout shift (CLS), optimized images, and minimal client-side JS.
- **Trust Signals:** Adding "ATS-Verified" badges, "100% Free" guarantees, and professional design cues.
- **Internal Linking:** Creating a "Career Hub" feel by linking to related AI tools (LinkedIn Bio, Interview Prep).

## 4. Local SEO Signals

- **Geo-Keywords:** Integrating "Pakistan", "Dubai", "UAE", and "GCC" naturally into the content and metadata.
- **Regional Formatting:** Mentioning specific regional requirements (e.g., photo requirements in GCC vs. US).
- **Local Schema:** Using `areaServed` in JSON-LD to target specific regions.
